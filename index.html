Our Commitment to Your Privacy
SuperFocusFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Effective Date: October 16, 2025
Last Updated: October 16, 2025
Information We Collect
We do NOT collect any personal information.

SuperFocusFlow operates completely offline. All your data including tasks, timer sessions, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not:
  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts or photos
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share data with third parties
  • Store data on remote servers
How We Use Your Information
Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to use, share, or sell. All your productivity data (tasks, pomodoro sessions, statistics, preferences) stays on your iPhone and is never transmitted anywhere.
Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored locally on your device using:
  • Core Data: For tasks and session history
  • UserDefaults: For app settings and preferences

This data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device.
Third-Party Services
SuperFocusFlow does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services. The app functions completely independently.
Children's Privacy
SuperFocusFlow does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my timer durations?
Navigate to Settings and adjust your preferences. Set focus sessions from 5-60 minutes, short breaks from 1-15 minutes, and long breaks from 5-30 minutes. Your settings are saved automatically.
How do I add widgets to my home screen?
Long press on your home screen, tap the "+" icon in the top corner, search for "SuperFocusFlow", select your preferred widget size (Small for progress circle, Medium for task + timer), and tap "Add Widget". The widget updates in real-time!
Is my data synced to the cloud?
Currently, all your data (tasks, statistics, settings) is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy and security. Your information never leaves your device. Cloud sync may be added in future updates based on user feedback.
Is SuperFocusFlow really 100% free?
Absolutely! Every single feature is completely free with zero ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases. We built SuperFocusFlow to help people achieve their goals, not to monetize features. It will stay free forever.
How do I change the timer visualization?
Tap on the timer display on the main screen to cycle through all 7 beautiful visualization styles: Circular, Liquid Fill, Digital Flip, Minimal, Animated, Particle, and Neon. Your preference is saved automatically.
How do I play ambient sounds during my focus sessions?
Tap the sound icon on the main screen to access our curated ambient sounds library. Choose from 10+ options including Rain, Ocean, Forest, Coffee Shop, and more. Adjust the volume or mute as needed. Sounds continue playing in the background during sessions.
Can I reset my statistics and start fresh?
Yes! Go to Settings and scroll down to find "Reset Statistics". This will clear all your productivity data, streak counts, and history. Please note: This action cannot be undone, so make sure you're ready for a fresh start.
What is the Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. Work in 25-minute focused sessions (pomodoros), take a 5-minute break, and after 4 pomodoros, take a longer 15-30 minute break. This helps maintain focus and prevent burnout.
Will there be more features in future updates?
Yes! We're actively developing new features based on user feedback. Potential additions include cloud sync across devices, additional language support, more timer visualizations, enhanced analytics, and deeper customization options. Send us your feature requests!
